Level: House of the Tyrant

3D Level using UE4

Summary

The House of The Tyrant is a 3D level based on the One Page Dungeon made by Michael Prescott for his Trilemma Adventures blog.
The idea behind this level was to adapt the dungeon scheme into 3D using UE4, adding some knowledge of the level design lessons learned in class, such as player guidance techniques, the critical path, the golden path... It started as a greybox using the basic shapes features of UE4(BSP),
and it evolved to what is now by adding level design techniques, props that fitted the style of the level and lots of polish.

My role in the project

Since this project has been developed by me and another student, I participated in almost everything involved with it. But most of all I enjoyed a lot working on the player guidance of the level.
