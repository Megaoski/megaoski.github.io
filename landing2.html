Fantasy Brawl

2D Batlle Royale game

Summary

Fantasy Brawl is a Battle Royale game developed by 8 students that features intense action in a PvP map. 4 players have to give their best in order to survive the struggle! Moreover, each player is in control of a totally different hero each with its own characteristics and special ability! Get ready warrior! A fierce battle against the strongest is about to begin! Every round won gives you 3 stars, and every kill 1 star. Play the 4 rounds and get the highest number of stars to win!

My role in the project

During the development of Fantasy Brawl I was in charge of designing and developing the UI of the game. I worked not only on the Menus and the Champion Selection Screen but also in the player HUD's
