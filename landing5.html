Raiden

Arcade game based on Raiden

Summary

This project is an tribute to the game Arcade game Raiden(1990). It has been developed by a small team of 4 people during 4 months. The game includes 2 levels with all the original enemies and bosses but we have included some modifications such as the posibility of 2 players and a full HD rework of the pixel art and animations.

My role in the project

Since for this project we were a small team of 4 and we had to deliver the game in 4 months, I worked on almost all of the areas of the game, minus the art rework of the original Raiden sprites.
