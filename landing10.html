Choosing to See: Designing Accessible Video Games for Visually Impaired Players

My End of Degree Project about making video games visually accessible

Summary

How do blind people play video games? How does Accessibility help players with vision problems? Do you want to know how to create more visually accesible video games? You can find the answer to all this questions and more here.

Living in the 21st century in an advanced and technologic world where even VR games can be played at home, not having proper Accessibility options for the players seems wrong. The goal of this paper is to inform and educate others and myself on a topic that must concern every developer and that should be something to take into account from the beginning of the development: how to make video games more accessible for visually impaired players.
